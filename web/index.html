AI Fact Checker Help How to use

Enter any statement and our AI will verify its accuracy using reliable sources.

You'll receive:

  • Classification (True/False/Uncertain)
  • Detailed explanation
  • Supporting evidence
  • Source verification

Click on any verified source to generate a summary:

  • Select your preferred language
  • Click "Generate" to get the summary
  • View the translated summary in a popup

Note: Only verified sources (green) can be summarized

Translate explanations to your preferred language:

  • Click the "Translate" button below any explanation
  • Choose your target language
  • View the translated text in a popup

For best results:

  • Be specific in your statements
  • Include dates and names when relevant
  • Keep statements focused on a single claim
  • Avoid complex or compound statements
